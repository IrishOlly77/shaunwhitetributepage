Shaun White

Shaun White is undoubtedly the King of snowboarding.

Here is a list of Shauns medal acheivments:

Winter Olympics

  • 2006 - Torino, Italy - Halfpipe - Gold

    • Shauns first Winter Olympic Gold medal with a winning score of 46.8 (out of 50).



  • 2010 - Vancover, Canada - Halfpipe - Gold

    • Unveiling his new trick 'The Tomahawk' (Double McTwist 1260).



  • 2014 - Sochi, Russia - Halfpipe - 4th

    • Even though White was not on the medal podium, he was still the most talked about athelte at the games



  • 2018 - PyeongChang, South Korea - Halfpipe - Gold

    Going into the third and final run, while also trailing Japans Ayumu Hirano, Shaun White lands back to back 1440s to secure gold.
